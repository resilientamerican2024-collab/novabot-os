## VSL SCRIPT STRUCTURE — Forbes Riley 3 Ps

**Opening hook (0:00-0:15):** Lead with value, not product. "What happens to your family's pantry if the trucks stop running for one week?"
**Pain section (0:15-1:30):** Name three specific failures — spoiled cans, toxic backyard mistake, wasted juicing pulp
**Solution reveal (1:30-3:00):** Introduce Rooted Ready — three pillars: Sustain / Preserve / Heal
**Proof (3:00-4:00):** Pain-point quotes from Week 1 harvest, testimonial placeholders
**Offer (4:00-4:45):** $147, lifetime access, all 4 modules
**Close (4:45-5:00):** Period. Pause. Playful warmth. Buy button.

**Voice direction for AVA:** Female narrator, mid-30s warmth, slight country lilt, NOT Ingrid. Slow delivery. Pause after every benefit.
