## WHO TANNIE IS

Tannie is a Latina woman in her late 20s. Her face is an AI blend of 
her mother (Ingrid K Santana) and her aunt (Rhianna), representing 
the spirit of her missing sister Kassandra.

Tannie survived a near-death attack after discovering her 
narcissistic boyfriend Job's betrayal at a party. Someone drugged 
her drink. She was attacked, left unconscious. During that 
unconsciousness, Jesus visited her and offered a second chance:

&gt; "You can't come near me right now because you're not dead. I'm 
&gt; showing you what could happen if you don't choose me. You have 
&gt; a chance to go back and choose different. Choose me. And while 
&gt; you're choosing me, show the world what amazing things I have 
&gt; already created."

She took six months of silence to heal, study the Bible, and 
reinvent herself. She emerged as a traveler, content creator, and 
faith-sharer. Now she explores the world with Princess Kay 
(Kassandra's dog), bringing God's word to others through story, 
fashion, and everyday life.

---

## PRINCESS KAY — KASSANDRA'S DOG

Princess Kay is NOT just a mascot. She was Kassandra's real 
companion. She is Kassandra's memory made visible. She goes 
everywhere with Tannie.

**Physical Description:**
- Small white fluffy dog (Maltese/Shih Tzu type)
- TRUE RED harness with SILVER hardware (functional, not decorative)
- Innocent, wide-eyed, toddler-like expression
- NEVER angry, NEVER mature-looking, NEVER wearing pink or costume 
  jewelry

**Emotional Role:**
- The heart of every story
- Relatable, funny, vulnerable
- Carries Kassandra's spirit forward

## THE SACRED COLORS

These colors are NOT aesthetic choices. They are theological 
statements. Every image MUST honor them.

| Color | Meaning | Visual Rule |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **RED** | Blood of Jesus — the sacrifice that saved Tannie | Deep true red ONLY. NO pink, NO coral, NO bright candy red. Nails, harness accents, dress details |
| **SILVER** | Refining, purification, cleansing | Subtle and functional. Hardware, cross necklace, rings. NEVER decorative bling or costume jewelry |
| **IVORY/OFF-WHITE** | Not perfect yet — aspiring to be like Jesus | Warm, textured backgrounds, silk robes, paper tones. NEVER cold white or sterile gray |
| **BLACK** | Grounding, ink lines, text bars | For outlines and text. Never overwhelming |

## VISUAL STYLE RULES

### For Story Panels (5-Panel Carousel):
- Comic book illustration with sketchy ink lines
- Subtle halftone dots in corners (NOT overwhelming)
- Warm textured paper background
- Princess Kay must be in every panel or series
- Tannie shown from behind or partial until "reveal" moment

### For Tannie Portraits (Real/Canonical):
- Photorealistic, warm lighting
- Silver cross necklace visible
- Red dress or ivory/cream outfit
- Confident, radiant, post-transformation glow
- The face: blend of Ingrid + Rhianna = Kassandra's spirit

### NEVER ALLOWED:
- Pink or coral instead of true red
- Princess Kay looking ferocious or mature
- Tannie in revealing clothing
- Costume jewelry or decorative pearls on Princess Kay
- Cold white backgrounds
- Preachy or doctrinal text
- Anything that dishonors Kassandra's memory
